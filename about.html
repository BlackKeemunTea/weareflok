Our story

Work should fit
around life.
Not the other way.

Flōk exists because the job board experience is broken. Buried salaries. Vague flexibility. Inclusive hiring as an afterthought. We're building something better.

"The tools for finding a job haven't kept up with the way people actually want to work."

The remote work revolution changed everything — except the job boards. They still list salaries as "competitive". They still let employers call a role "flexible" with no accountability. They still make it harder than it needs to be to find work that genuinely fits your life.

Flōk was built to change that. We're a UK-first platform for remote and flexible jobs — with inclusion baked in from day one, salary transparency as a non-negotiable, and a community of job seekers who deserve better than the status quo.

We believe that access to good, flexible work is an equity issue. The people who most need flexibility — carers, people with disabilities, those outside major cities — are also the ones most disadvantaged by opaque listings and inaccessible hiring processes. Flōk is for them too.

What we stand for
Our commitments
01
Transparency, always
Salary shown on every listing. No exceptions. No "DOE". No "competitive". Just numbers.
02
Signals, not claims
We surface what listings actually say about flexibility and inclusion — no inflated promises.
03
Access for everyone
Good remote work shouldn't be a privilege. Job seekers use Flōk free — always, no exceptions.
04
Community over scale
We'd rather be the most trusted job board in the UK than the biggest. Quality always wins.
